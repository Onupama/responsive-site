Krishna and Balarama by Jamini Roy

Jamini Roy

Jamini Roy was an Indian painter best known for combining traditional Indian and Western art styles to create unique and complex works. Inspired by local art forms like patachitra and Kalighat pat, his work is characterized by bold lines, vibrant colors, and themes from rural life, mythology, and religious stories.
